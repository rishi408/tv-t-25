1. Live Bus Tracking

You can view the real-time location of college buses on the map. To do this:

  • Go to the Live Tracking tab on the homepage.
  • Select your route or bus number.
  • See the current location and estimated arrival time.

2. Bus Schedule

Check daily and weekly schedules:

  • Click on the Schedule menu.
  • Choose your campus and route.
  • View departure and arrival times.

3. Notifications

Enable notifications to get updates about:

  • Bus delays or route changes.
  • Arrival reminders.
